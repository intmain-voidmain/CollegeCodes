Shah Rukh Khan, also known as SRK, is one of the most popular and successful actors in the Indian film industry. He is often referred to as the "King of Bollywood" and has acted in over 80 Bollywood films. He made his acting debut in 1992 and has since then received numerous awards and accolades for his performances, including 14 Film Fair Awards and several other international awards. SRK is known for his charismatic screen presence, versatility as an actor, and his romantic roles in films such as "Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge," "Kuch Kuch Hota Hai," and "Kal Ho Naa Ho." He has also played a variety of characters, including negative roles, and is widely regarded as one of the finest actors in the Indian film industry. In addition to his acting career, SRK is also a successful businessman and has co-owned the Kolkata Knight Riders team in the Indian Premier League. He is also a philanthropist and has been involved in several charitable initiatives through his Meer Foundation, which focuses on empowering women and children. SRK continues to be one of the most popular and influential figures in Indian entertainment and is widely loved by fans all over the world.
